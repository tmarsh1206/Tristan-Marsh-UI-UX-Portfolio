Behavioral Demographics + Drive

She seeks opportunities for personal development, mindfulness, and self-improvement.

Goals & Needs

Support Fundraising Campaigns: Emily is interested in participating in fundraising campaigns and events organized by UNICEF USA. She wants to know about ongoing campaigns, their impact, and how to contribute to them.

Hesitations & Pain Points

Transparency and Impact: Emily seeks clear information about how her contributions are making a direct impact on children's lives. She wants to see tangible outcomes and success stories related to helping people in need.

We’re better because of the amount of donations received to help children all across America. We’re believable because we have a huge network of repeat donors who continue to support our cause.
