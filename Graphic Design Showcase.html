2. Define

The Problem

We have observed that local artists face significant challenges when seeking commission work, often struggling to find suitable opportunities that align with their creative style and preferences. Existing job-board platforms lack tailored features, leading to frustration as artists sift through irrelevant listings and contend with limited search filters. Communication hurdles arise due to inconsistent messaging systems, hindering effective collaboration between artists and clients. Additionally, the absence of a cohesive platform for showcasing portfolios and personalized pitches leaves artists spending excessive time tailoring their submissions for each project. This disjointed process detracts from their core artistic focus and undermines their ability to connect with local businesses and customers seeking their expertise. A solution is needed to address these pain points, providing a user-centric platform that empowers artists to effortlessly discover, communicate, and showcase their skills, enabling them to fully embrace their creative potential.
