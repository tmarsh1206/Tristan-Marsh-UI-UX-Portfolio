2. Information Architecture

Annotated Agency Navigation

Usability Website Navigation Tests

User testing on ed.gov revealed that while the Header Navigation Tabs were useful, users often didn’t know what each tab contained until clicked. The Footer was comprehensive but lacked clear hierarchy, making it somewhat confusing to navigate. The Search bar was helpful but sometimes led to external pages, causing confusion. The sidebar’s lists were particularly problematic, as users had to click "more" and still found the labels unclear. Overall, users managed to complete most tasks but felt the site was outdated and would benefit from a redesign of its navigation and content.
